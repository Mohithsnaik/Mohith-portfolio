Credit Card Financial Dashboard

  • Developed a comprehensive Credit Card Financial Dashboard using Power BI, offering deep insights into customer spending behavior, revenue trends, and transaction patterns across demographics and card categories.
  • Customer Report: Visualized and analyzed revenue segmented by education level, income group, marital status, and number of dependents, uncovering spending patterns and enabling data-driven marketing strategies.
  • Identified top-performing regions by analyzing data from the top 5 states, providing insights into geographic trends in credit card usage and revenue generation.
  • Created an age-group analysis dashboard to highlight variations in revenue and spending behavior among different customer age segments.
  • Card Transaction Report: Calculated revenue and transaction volumes by card category (Blue, Gold, Platinum, Silver), education level, and expenditure type (Bills, Entertainment, Food, Fuel, Grocery, Travel) to reveal customer preferences and spending habits.
  • Analyzed chip adoption trends by tracking revenue and transaction data for users with and without chip-enabled cards, uncovering the benefits and business implications of chip usage.
  • Visualized transaction volume by quantity to identify high-value transactions, enabling businesses to optimize offerings and identify areas for growth or improvement.
  • Utilized Power BI for data integration, transformation, and interactive dashboards. Applied DAX for advanced metrics and actionable insights into customer and transaction data.
