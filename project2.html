Pizza Sales Dashboard Analysis

  • Developed a comprehensive Pizza Sales Dashboard using Power BI, delivering actionable insights into sales performance, customer preferences, and product demand across categories, time frames, and pizza attributes.
  • Sales Overview: Displayed key metrics such as total revenue, order volume, and average order value using card visuals. Leveraged area and column charts to analyze sales trends over time, enabling performance monitoring and seasonal planning.
  • Category Analysis: Visualized sales distribution by pizza category (e.g., Classic, Supreme, Veggie) and ingredient types using donut and funnel charts, revealing customer preferences and top-performing segments.
  • Time-Based Performance: Integrated date and time slicers to analyze sales patterns across different time periods (daily, weekly, monthly), supporting promotional planning and demand forecasting.
  • Best and Worst Sellers Report: Identified top-selling and underperforming pizzas using ranked bar charts, helping optimize inventory, streamline menu offerings, and align with customer demand.
  • Product Insights: Compared sales by pizza size and type, uncovering trends in customer choices and supporting strategic decisions around pricing, combo deals, and marketing efforts.
  • Utilized Power BI’s data modeling and visualization capabilities for dynamic reporting. Applied DAX functions for advanced calculations including revenue metrics, ranking logic, and filter-based measures.
  • Designed an interactive and user-friendly dashboard with clear navigation between performance overview and product-level analytics, empowering stakeholders to make data-informed business decisions.
