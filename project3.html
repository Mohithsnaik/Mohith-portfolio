Bank Customer Churn Analysis

  • Designed a comprehensive Bank Churn Analysis Dashboard using Power BI to monitor customer retention, analyze churn patterns, and support strategic decision-making.
  • Customer Overview: Displayed key KPIs using card and gauge visuals to highlight overall churn rate, active customer count, and benchmark thresholds for stakeholder visibility.
  • Demographic Segmentation: Implemented donut charts to segment churn distribution across gender, geography, tenure, and customer categories, uncovering at-risk groups for targeted retention efforts.
  • Behavioral Analysis: Leveraged line and column combo charts to track churn trends over time and correlate them with product usage or service subscriptions, identifying behavior-based churn triggers.
  • Interactive Filtering: Enabled dynamic analysis through slicers for product type, geography, and customer tenure, empowering business users to explore trends with ease.
  • Churn Drivers: Integrated visual comparisons and distribution charts to explore potential drivers of churn such as low engagement, limited product holdings, or service dissatisfaction.
  • Applied advanced DAX calculations to compute churn rate, retention ratio, and customer tenure metrics, enabling deeper analytical insights and predictive indicators.
  • Designed a user-friendly, visually intuitive dashboard layout with layered elements and clear hierarchy to ensure accessibility and stakeholder adoption.
