Music Store Analysis

  • Developed a comprehensive SQL Music Store Analysis project using PostgreSQL and PgAdmin to explore sales trends, customer preferences, and artist performance within a digital music platform.
  • Leadership & Team Structure: Identified the most senior employee in the company using ranking functions, providing insights into the organization's hierarchy.
  • Market & Revenue Analysis: Determined the United States as the top-performing country by invoice volume, and Prague as the highest revenue-generating city—ideal for promotional focus.
  • Customer Insights: Ranked top-spending customers globally and by country. Revealed a high-value customer base concentrated in Prague, guiding loyalty campaign strategies.
  • Genre & Listener Segmentation: Discovered Rock as the most popular genre. Extracted listener details by genre and region to enable targeted outreach and tailored marketing.
  • Artist Performance: Analyzed top-performing artists in the Rock genre by total track count, revealing highly engaged contributors to the platform's content catalog.
  • Track Duration Analysis: Identified songs exceeding the average length, offering opportunities for niche promotion in genres like instrumental or classical music.
  • Spending Patterns by Region: Used advanced SQL queries (with CTEs and window functions) to find the top-spending customers per country, informing regional sales and engagement strategies.
  • Utilized complex SQL concepts such as JOINs, subqueries, window functions, aggregations, and CTEs to drive end-to-end business intelligence reporting and support strategic decisions.
  • Business Recommendations:
    • Host a high-profile music event in Prague, given its leadership in revenue and customer value.
    • Launch direct marketing campaigns targeting Rock genre listeners using their contact data.
    • Tailor music offerings and promotions based on country-specific genre preferences.
    • Offer premium memberships or loyalty perks to top customers in each region.
    • Promote long-duration tracks through specialized channels (e.g., classical, ambient playlists).
